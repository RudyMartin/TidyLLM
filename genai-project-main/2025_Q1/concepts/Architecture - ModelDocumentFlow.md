
### Proposed Flow for Model Document Pre-Review

1. **User Login and Setup**:
   - Users enter their name to personalize the session.
   - Optional: Confirm or update user-specific settings such as preferred topics or document sections.

2. **Topic and Section Selection**:
   - Users select a general topic related to their query (e.g., enterprise policies, model standards).
   - Users can specify document sections they are particularly interested in, such as the methodology or results sections of reports.

3. **Query Input**:
   - Users input their specific query, which the system will use to retrieve relevant documents and sections.
   - This step is crucial as it directs the search engine on what content to focus on within the vast array of documents.

4. **Document Retrieval and Section Highlighting**:
   - The system performs a search based on the query and the selected topic/sections.
   - Results are displayed with not just links to documents but also highlights or excerpts from sections that are particularly relevant to the query.

5. **User Interaction with Results**:
   - Users can review the returned document sections directly within the app.
   - They can provide feedback on each document and section (like/dislike), which helps refine future searches and recommendations.

6. **Document Adjustment Suggestions**:
   - Based on user interactions and preferences, suggest adjustments or further readings.
   - Offer tools to annotate or comment on document sections directly within the interface, preparing for a comprehensive review or follow-up actions.

7. **Settings and Preferences Management**:
   - Allow users to save their settings and preferences for future sessions, enabling a more tailored experience each time they use the app.

8. **Exit or Further Actions**:
   - After completing their review or when they choose to end the session, users can either close the app, save their session for later continuation, or start a new query.

### Evaluation of Flow

- **Ease of Use**: The flow is designed to be user-friendly, with clear, guided steps that logically lead from one to the next.
- **Completeness**: Each step builds upon the previous to ensure that users not only find the documents they need but also get actionable insights from their contents.
- **Efficiency**: The integration of user preferences and feedback loops helps streamline the search process, making the app more responsive to individual needs over time.

### Possible Enhancements

- **Machine Learning Integration**: Use machine learning to predict and automatically select sections of documents that users frequently find useful based on their past interactions.
- **Collaborative Features**: Add functionalities for users to share their findings or collaborate on document reviews directly within the app.

### Naming the Process

Calling this process a "Model Document Pre-Review" seems appropriate as it clearly indicates the purpose—preparing and reviewing documents based on a model or set standard. This term could help set the correct expectations for users about what the tool is intended to achieve.
