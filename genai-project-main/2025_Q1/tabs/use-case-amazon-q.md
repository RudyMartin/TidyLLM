**Short Requirement (for Amazon Q Business on  QuickSight)**

We need a solution that leverages *Amazon QuickSight Q* to automatically analyze and summarize unstructured documentation (such as model development notes, risk findings, and governance paperwork). The system should:

1. **Ingest & Classify Documents:** Collect files from various sources (PDFs, Word docs, etc.) and categorize them by topic (e.g., “Model Architecture,” “Validation Findings,” “Compliance Risks”).  
2. **Generate Summaries & Insights:** Use NLP to create concise text summaries highlighting key themes, metrics, risks, and recommendations for each document cluster.  
3. **Integrate into QuickSight Q:** Present these AI-generated insights within QuickSight Q dashboards, enabling stakeholders to query, view, and quickly act on the most critical information without manually reading all documentation.  
4. **Facilitate Governance & Compliance:** Automatically flag potential policy issues, missing approvals, or process gaps and propose next steps in plain language.
