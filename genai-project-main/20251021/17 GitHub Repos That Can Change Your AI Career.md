

# 🚀 17 GitHub Repos That Can Change Your AI Career

*Post originally by [Sairam Sundaresan](https://www.linkedin.com/in/sairamsundaresan/) — AI Engineering Leader, Author of **AI for the Rest of Us***

If you're serious about AI this year, these repositories are worth bookmarking 👇

---

1. **[ML-For-Beginners](https://github.com/microsoft/ML-For-Beginners)**
   ↳ 12-week curriculum. Classic ML. Project-based. Quiz-backed.

2. **[ML Roadmap](https://github.com/mrdbourke/machine-learning-roadmap)**
   ↳ A no-fluff ML roadmap built for self-learners.

3. **[Neural Networks: Zero to Hero](https://github.com/karpathy/nn-zero-to-hero)**
   ↳ Build neural nets from scratch. Understand every step.

4. **[Awesome Computer Vision](https://github.com/jbhuang0604/awesome-computer-vision)**
   ↳ The best CV collection: books, datasets, research tips, projects.

5. **[Awesome NLP](https://github.com/keon/awesome-nlp)**
   ↳ From transformers to multilingual NLP tools, plus top courses.

6. **[Hands-on LLMs](https://github.com/rasbt/hands-on-llms)**
   ↳ Understand LLMs from the ground up with curated notebooks.

7. **[Prompt Engineering Guide](https://github.com/dair-ai/Prompt-Engineering-Guide)**
   ↳ Everything prompt engineering under the sun in one place.

8. **[Awesome Data Science](https://github.com/academic/awesome-datascience)**
   ↳ The ultimate free resource list: books, courses, datasets, tools.

9. **[All RL Algorithms](https://github.com/dennybritz/reinforcement-learning)**
   ↳ Clean RL implementations with a cheat sheet.

10. **[Awesome RL](https://github.com/aikorea/awesome-rl)**
    ↳ A rich archive of RL theory, papers, demos, and apps.

11. **[Awesome Generative AI](https://github.com/steven2358/awesome-generative-ai)**
    ↳ The most current GenAI list: courses, tools, and papers by recency.

12. **[AI Agents for Beginners](https://github.com/microsoft/AI-Agents-for-Beginners)**
    ↳ Jump into agentic systems with a free course.

13. **[Advanced RAG Techniques](https://github.com/NirDiamant/awesome-RAG)**
    ↳ Understand the bones of RAG and become a retrieval master.

14. **[Gen-AI Agents](https://github.com/e2b-dev/awesome-genai-agents)**
    ↳ Learn how to build GenAI agents, tools, and APIs.

15. **[Made with ML](https://github.com/GokuMohandas/Made-With-ML)**
    ↳ The definitive MLOps guide for any practitioner.

16. **[Annotated AI Paper Implementations](https://github.com/labmlai/annotated_deep_learning_paper_implementations)**
    ↳ 60+ papers with inline code notes including transformers, optimizers, and GANs from the inside out.
