Picking a model
For “robot dog” experiments and general embodied‑AI work, the Go2 (consumer/ent/edu/edu+) is the usual starting point, with options for extra compute (Jetson Orin), 3D LiDAR, and even an arm.
​

For manipulation or humanoid‑style research, the G1 or H1 lines offer bimanual capabilities and higher costs, often sold via education/enterprise channels like RoboWorks or dealers rather than just the basic shop page.
