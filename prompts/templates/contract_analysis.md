# Contract Analysis Workflow Template
*Comprehensive contract review and risk assessment*

## Contract Classification and Overview
```
Contract Document Classification:

Analyze the contract/agreement and provide comprehensive classification:

Contract Document: {document_content}

Classification Analysis:
1. **Contract Type**: Identify specific contract category (employment, service, NDA, purchase, lease, etc.)
2. **Parties Analysis**: 
   - Primary contracting parties
   - Third-party beneficiaries
   - Subsidiary/affiliate relationships
   - Authority and capacity assessment
3. **Contract Scope**: Services, products, or relationships covered
4. **Jurisdiction**: Governing law, venue, dispute resolution
5. **Document Quality**: Completeness, clarity, standard compliance
6. **Risk Profile**: Initial risk assessment (High/Medium/Low)

Output structured analysis suitable for legal and business review.
```

## Key Terms and Conditions Analysis
```
Contract Terms and Conditions Breakdown:

Detailed analysis of critical contract provisions:

Contract Content: {document_content}

Terms Analysis:
1. **Financial Terms**:
   - Payment amounts, schedules, methods
   - Penalties, late fees, interest rates
   - Currency, tax responsibilities
   - Budget caps, cost escalation clauses

2. **Performance Obligations**:
   - Deliverables and specifications
   - Performance standards and metrics
   - Timelines and milestones
   - Acceptance criteria

3. **Risk Allocation**:
   - Liability limitations and caps
   - Indemnification provisions
   - Insurance requirements
   - Force majeure clauses

4. **Term and Termination**:
   - Contract duration and renewal
   - Termination triggers and procedures
   - Post-termination obligations
   - Survival clauses

Highlight unusual, unfavorable, or high-risk provisions.
```

## Compliance and Risk Assessment
```
Contract Compliance and Risk Evaluation:

Comprehensive risk assessment and compliance check:

Contract for Review: {document_content}

Risk Assessment:
1. **Legal Compliance**:
   - Regulatory requirement adherence
   - Industry standard compliance
   - Licensing and certification needs
   - Data protection and privacy laws

2. **Business Risk Factors**:
   - Financial exposure assessment
   - Operational risk identification
   - Reputational risk considerations
   - Strategic alignment evaluation

3. **Performance Risks**:
   - Delivery capability assessment
   - Resource requirement analysis
   - Timeline feasibility review
   - Quality assurance provisions

4. **Mitigation Strategies**:
   - Risk mitigation recommendations
   - Required amendments or clarifications
   - Additional protections needed
   - Monitoring and oversight requirements

Provide risk matrix with severity and probability assessments.
```

## Contract Negotiation and Decision Support
```
Contract Decision Support Analysis:

Strategic analysis to support contract negotiations and approval decisions:

Contract Under Consideration: {document_content}

Decision Support:
1. **Business Case Analysis**:
   - Strategic value and alignment
   - Cost-benefit evaluation
   - Alternative options comparison
   - Market competitiveness assessment

2. **Negotiation Priorities**:
   - Must-have vs. nice-to-have terms
   - Deal-breaker identification
   - Negotiation leverage assessment
   - Compromise strategies

3. **Approval Recommendations**:
   - Recommend: Approve, Reject, or Negotiate
   - Key concerns requiring resolution
   - Required stakeholder approvals
   - Implementation readiness assessment

4. **Next Steps**:
   - Immediate actions required
   - Negotiation strategy recommendations
   - Due diligence requirements
   - Timeline for decision-making

Format as executive briefing for senior management decision-making.
```
