# Financial Analysis Workflow Template
*Comprehensive financial document analysis and risk assessment*

## Financial Document Classification
```
Financial Document Analysis and Classification:

Analyze and classify the financial document for appropriate review workflow:

Financial Document: {document_content}

Document Classification:
1. **Document Type**:
   - Financial statements (income, balance sheet, cash flow)
   - Credit reports and assessments
   - Loan applications and documentation
   - Investment proposals and analyses
   - Budget and forecasting documents

2. **Financial Scope**:
   - Individual vs. corporate analysis
   - Document time period and frequency
   - Currency and accounting standards
   - Audit status and certification

3. **Stakeholder Context**:
   - Primary users and decision makers
   - Regulatory reporting requirements
   - Business purpose and objectives
   - Risk tolerance and parameters

4. **Data Quality Assessment**:
   - Completeness of financial information
   - Data accuracy and verification
   - Supporting documentation availability
   - Compliance with standards

Output comprehensive financial document profile for appropriate analysis workflow.
```

## Financial Performance Analysis
```
Financial Performance and Health Assessment:

Detailed analysis of financial performance metrics and trends:

Financial Data: {document_content}

Performance Analysis:
1. **Financial Ratios**:
   - Liquidity ratios (current, quick, cash)
   - Profitability ratios (margin, ROE, ROA)
   - Leverage ratios (debt-to-equity, interest coverage)
   - Efficiency ratios (turnover, utilization)

2. **Trend Analysis**:
   - Year-over-year comparisons
   - Seasonal pattern identification
   - Growth trajectory assessment
   - Performance consistency evaluation

3. **Benchmark Comparison**:
   - Industry standard comparisons
   - Peer company analysis
   - Market performance relative positioning
   - Best-in-class benchmarking

4. **Financial Health Score**:
   - Composite financial strength rating
   - Risk indicator assessment
   - Sustainability evaluation
   - Credit worthiness implications

Provide comprehensive financial scorecard with trend analysis and benchmarking.
```

## Risk Assessment and Credit Analysis
```
Financial Risk Assessment and Credit Evaluation:

Comprehensive risk analysis for lending, investment, or partnership decisions:

Financial Information: {document_content}

Risk Assessment:
1. **Credit Risk Evaluation**:
   - Default probability assessment
   - Payment history and patterns
   - Debt service capability analysis
   - Collateral and security evaluation

2. **Business Risk Factors**:
   - Industry and market risks
   - Operational risk assessment
   - Management quality evaluation
   - Competitive position analysis

3. **Financial Risk Indicators**:
   - Cash flow stability and predictability
   - Capital structure sustainability
   - Working capital management
   - Financial flexibility assessment

4. **Risk Mitigation**:
   - Risk mitigation strategies
   - Covenant and monitoring requirements
   - Collateral and guarantee needs
   - Pricing and terms recommendations

Format as comprehensive credit memo with clear risk rating and recommendations.
```

## Investment and Decision Support
```
Financial Decision Support Analysis:

Strategic financial analysis to support investment and business decisions:

Financial Analysis Subject: {document_content}

Decision Support:
1. **Investment Analysis**:
   - Return on investment projections
   - Net present value calculations
   - Payback period analysis
   - Risk-adjusted return assessment

2. **Scenario Analysis**:
   - Best case, base case, worst case scenarios
   - Sensitivity analysis on key variables
   - Monte Carlo risk simulations
   - Break-even analysis

3. **Strategic Implications**:
   - Strategic fit and alignment
   - Market opportunity assessment
   - Competitive advantage evaluation
   - Resource requirement analysis

4. **Recommendation Framework**:
   - Go/No-go recommendation with rationale
   - Alternative structuring options
   - Terms and conditions recommendations
   - Monitoring and review requirements

Present as executive financial briefing with clear decision framework and recommendations.
```
