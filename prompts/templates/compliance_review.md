# Compliance Review Workflow Template
*Regulatory compliance analysis and risk assessment*

## Regulatory Compliance Classification
```
Compliance Document Classification:

Identify regulatory framework and compliance requirements for the document:

Document for Compliance Review: {document_content}

Compliance Classification:
1. **Regulatory Framework**:
   - Primary regulations applicable (FCRA, Basel III, SOX, GDPR, etc.)
   - Secondary regulations and standards
   - Industry-specific requirements
   - Jurisdictional considerations

2. **Compliance Scope**:
   - Document type and business purpose
   - Compliance areas affected
   - Stakeholder impact assessment
   - Risk categorization

3. **Compliance Maturity**:
   - Current compliance posture
   - Gap assessment from requirements
   - Historical compliance performance
   - Improvement opportunities

4. **Regulatory Environment**:
   - Recent regulatory changes
   - Upcoming compliance deadlines
   - Industry enforcement trends
   - Regulatory relationship status

Output comprehensive compliance framework analysis.
```

## Compliance Requirements Analysis
```
Detailed Compliance Requirements Assessment:

Map specific regulatory requirements against document content:

Document Content: {document_content}
Applicable Regulations: [Based on classification above]

Requirements Analysis:
1. **Mandatory Requirements**:
   - Must-have compliance elements
   - Non-negotiable regulatory standards
   - Critical control requirements
   - Reporting and documentation needs

2. **Gap Analysis**:
   - Missing required information
   - Incomplete compliance elements
   - Documentation deficiencies
   - Process or control gaps

3. **Risk Assessment**:
   - Compliance violation risks
   - Regulatory enforcement exposure
   - Financial penalty potential
   - Reputational impact assessment

4. **Remediation Requirements**:
   - Immediate actions needed
   - Long-term compliance improvements
   - Resource requirements
   - Timeline for compliance achievement

Provide detailed gap analysis with remediation roadmap.
```

## Risk and Impact Assessment
```
Compliance Risk and Business Impact Analysis:

Evaluate compliance risks and their business implications:

Document Under Review: {document_content}

Risk and Impact Analysis:
1. **Compliance Risk Matrix**:
   - High/Medium/Low risk categorization
   - Probability vs. impact assessment
   - Risk interdependencies
   - Regulatory scrutiny likelihood

2. **Business Impact**:
   - Operational disruption potential
   - Financial exposure (fines, penalties, costs)
   - Customer and stakeholder impact
   - Competitive advantage implications

3. **Regulatory Consequences**:
   - Enforcement action probability
   - Regulatory investigation triggers
   - Industry reputation effects
   - Cross-jurisdictional implications

4. **Mitigation Strategies**:
   - Risk mitigation options
   - Cost-benefit analysis of controls
   - Implementation feasibility
   - Ongoing monitoring requirements

Present as risk register with clear impact scenarios.
```

## Compliance Action Plan
```
Compliance Action Plan and Recommendations:

Develop actionable compliance improvement plan:

Current State Analysis: Based on document review of {document_content}

Action Plan:
1. **Immediate Actions** (0-30 days):
   - Critical compliance fixes
   - Emergency risk mitigation
   - Stakeholder notifications
   - Regulatory communications

2. **Short-term Actions** (30-90 days):
   - Process improvements
   - Documentation updates
   - Training requirements
   - System enhancements

3. **Long-term Strategy** (90+ days):
   - Structural compliance improvements
   - Technology investments
   - Organizational changes
   - Continuous monitoring programs

4. **Success Metrics**:
   - Compliance KPIs and targets
   - Measurement and reporting
   - Regular review schedules
   - Continuous improvement processes

Format as executable project plan with clear ownership and timelines.
```
