📊 Executive Summary

System Overview: The AI Model Compliance Assessment System provides automated evaluation of AI/ML model documentation against industry standards and regulatory requirements. This enterprise solution ensures models meet governance, risk, and compliance (GRC) standards before deployment.

Key Capabilities:

  • Automated compliance scoring against ISO 27001, GDPR, SOX, and NIST frameworks
  • Real-time document analysis with AI-powered content extraction
  • Risk assessment and mitigation recommendations
  • Regulatory mapping and audit trail generation
  • Executive dashboard with compliance metrics

Target Users: Chief Risk Officers, Compliance Officers, Data Scientists, Model Governance Teams, and Regulatory Affairs Specialists.

Compliance Frameworks: ISO 27001:2013, GDPR Article 5, SOX Section 404, NIST AI Risk Management, Basel III, IEEE 1012-2016, ISO/IEC 25010, COSO Framework, ITIL v4
